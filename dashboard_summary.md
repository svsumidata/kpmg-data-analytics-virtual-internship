## Objective
Develop a client-facing dashboard summary to present key insights and recommendations
based on the analysis of customer and transaction data.

---

## Dashboard Overview
The dashboard was designed to provide a clear, high-level view of customer behavior,
sales performance, and potential target customers for marketing.

Key focus areas:
- Customer demographics
- Purchase frequency and value
- High-value customer segments
- New customer targeting opportunities

---

## Key Insights
- A small percentage of customers contribute a large share of total revenue.
- Customers with higher purchase frequency show stronger long-term value.
- Certain age groups and regions show higher engagement.
- New customer list contains profiles similar to existing high-value customers.

---

## Business Recommendations
- Prioritize marketing campaigns toward high-value and frequent buyers.
- Target new customers that closely match top-performing customer profiles.
- Improve data consistency to enable better segmentation in future analyses.
- Use dashboards regularly for executive-level decision-making.

---

## Tools Used
- Excel (data exploration and preparation)
- Python (data quality checks)
- Data visualization tools (dashboard design concept)

---

## Outcome
This dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly understand performance trends and
make informed, data-driven business decisions.
